How to Use

  1. Upload Images: Click on the "Upload" button in the navigation bar or the central upload box. You can upload JPG, JPEG, PNG, or TIFF images. To select multiple images, hold down the `Ctrl` (Windows) or `Command` (Mac) key while clicking on the files. The system allows a maximum of 30 images at once.
  2. Review Uploaded Images: Once you have selected your images, you will see a preview of them in the "Extract Iris" section. Verify the images are correct and ready for processing.
  3. Extract Iris: Click on the "Extract Iris" button. The application will process your images and generate masks for each one. This may take some time depending on the number of images and their sizes.
  4. Processing Status: While the images are being processed, a loading indicator will be displayed. Please wait until the processing is complete.
  5. Download Results: Once processing is complete, you will see a success message. Your processed masks will be available for download in a ZIP file. The download will start automatically, but you can also click the provided link if needed.
  6. Upload More Images: To process additional images, click the "Upload More" button. This will return you to the upload section where you can start the process again.

About the Project


The idea for this project came from a challenge I faced during my internship. I was assigned a task involving iris segmentation, but at that time, I was new to using machine learning for such tasks. I struggled to get good results with the traditional computer vision methods I was trying.

I searched for an online iris segmentation tool to help me out, but I couldn't find one that met my needs. So, I decided to create my own solution. I developed a tool that quickly generates masks for the human iris from the images you provide. These masks are then ready for further processing and analysis.

What started as a personal need has now become a practical tool that fills a gap in the available solutions. This project reflects the journey from struggling with a problem to finding a solution that works effectively.
